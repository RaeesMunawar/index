Book Title

Summary

"Behave" by Dr. Robert Sapolsky is a comprehensive exploration of human behavior that dives deep into the intricacies of neuroscience, biology, and the social sciences. The book examines the multifaceted factors that shape human actions, ranging from genetics and hormones to cultural influences and societal structures. Dr. Sapolsky takes readers on a captivating journey through the various layers of the human brain, unraveling the complexities that underlie our behaviors and decision-making processes. The book provides a holistic understanding of what makes us who we are and why we behave the way we do.
